About

We are The Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers in GUC (IEEE GUC), a non-profit organization whose main goals are: Upgrading humanity and empowering GUC students with the extra needed technical and non-technical skills and knowledge to cope with the technical evolution in the recent years.

We have 10 different committees: 4 Technical and 6 OC, all working together to:

1) Transfer the technical knowledge of the IEEE innovation committee members through holding technical sessions , events and workshops.

2) Provide the technical support for the GUC students by supplying them with the hardware packages for their projects.

3) Publish our own research paper and videos to spread informative ideas to all of the GUC students.

4) Participate in different technical competitions to improve the members skills and experience through projects.

5) Create events and trips, focusing on improving the soft non-technical skills.
